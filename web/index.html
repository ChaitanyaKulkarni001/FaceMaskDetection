At Face Mask Detection System, we're dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology to enhance public safety and promote healthy environments. Our mission is to provide reliable and efficient solutions for the detection of face masks in various settings, contributing to the global effort to combat the spread of infectious diseases.

A face mask detector is like a smart tool that can see if people are wearing masks on their faces or not. It works using cameras or sensors to check if a person's face has a mask on it..

Here's how it works: Scanning Faces: The detector looks at faces to see if they have masks. Identification: It figures out if the face has a mask or not by analyzing certain features. Alerting: If it detects someone without a mask (when they should have one), it might alert someone, like a security guard or a system administrator.
